THOR Vector Graphics

Super Lightweight Engine

Thor Vector Graphics is an open-source, lightweight, and portable library designed for rendering vector-based scenes and animations, including SVG and Lottie formats. It offers user-friendly APIs, making it versatile for use across different software platforms and applications to visualize graphical content. As the vector graphics rendering engine for Tizen OS, Godot Platform, LVGL, Lottie Creator and the dotLottie player, ThorVG powers a wide range of products.
